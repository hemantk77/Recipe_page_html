The complete process to make the best chocolate cookies is right here...

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugars together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In another bowl, whisk the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips (and nuts if using).
  7. Scoop dough (about 1-2 tbsp per cookie) onto the baking tray, leaving some space between them.
  8. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until edges are lightly golden but centers are soft.
  9. Let cookies cool on the tray for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
